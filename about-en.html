About ThermaViX

Who are we?

ThermaViX is an Algerian startup founded by Master's graduates specializing in Biomedical Devices from Dr. Moulay Tahar University - Saïda. We aim to revolutionize medical diagnosis by developing smart solutions based on infrared thermal imaging and artificial intelligence.

Our goal is to provide advanced medical devices and technologies that help doctors and healthcare facilities in the early diagnosis of diseases, especially in resource-limited areas, with a focus on ease of use, safety, and high accuracy.

Our Vision

To become leaders in smart medical technologies in Algeria and the Arab world, and to make early thermal diagnosis accessible and easy for every healthcare facility and every patient.

Our Mission

To facilitate and encourage early disease diagnosis through modern, economical, and safe technology, while promoting research and development in the national health sector.

Our Values

  • Innovation and creativity in healthcare solutions
  • Accuracy and reliability in all our products
  • Commitment to facilitating healthcare for all
  • Collaboration and teamwork
  • Transparency and respect for medical ethics
