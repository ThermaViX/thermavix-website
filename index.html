Advanced Embedded Medical Thermography System

About ThermaViX

ThermaViX is a cutting-edge embedded system developed for medical infrared thermography applications. It leverages advanced thermal imaging sensors to assist healthcare professionals in early diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment by providing accurate, non-invasive thermal scans.

Why ThermaViX?

  • Accurate real-time thermal imaging
  • Non-contact medical diagnostics
  • Portable and embedded design
  • Cost-effective and easy to use
  • Applicable for vascular, oncological, and inflammation monitoring
