About ThermaViX

ThermaViX is a cutting-edge embedded system designed to revolutionize medical diagnostics through non-invasive infrared thermography. Our technology allows early detection of various diseases such as vascular disorders, tumors, inflammation, and metabolic imbalances.

With real-time thermal imaging, high precision sensors, and advanced analytics, ThermaViX empowers healthcare professionals to make better clinical decisions and improve patient outcomes.
