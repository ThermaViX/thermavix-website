How to Use ThermaViX

1
Setup & Calibration

Power on the device and allow 2-3 minutes for thermal sensor calibration. The software will automatically detect and connect to the device.

2
Patient Positioning

Position the patient 0.5-1 meter from the device. Ensure the target area is exposed and free from clothing or accessories.

3
Thermal Scanning

Start the scan through the software interface. The device captures real-time thermal data, displaying temperature variations instantly.

4
Review Results

Review the detailed analysis report, complete with thermal maps, temperature readings, and AI-generated health insights.

5
Generate Report

Create comprehensive reports for medical records, including thermal images, analysis data, and recommended follow-up actions.
