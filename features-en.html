Key Features of ThermaViX

Fast and Instant Diagnosis

The system allows thermal detection of diseases in a matter of seconds, saving time and increasing the speed of medical intervention.

Non-invasive and 100% Safe

Requires no patient contact or use of harmful radiation, completely safe for patients of all ages.

Advanced Artificial Intelligence

Relies on computer vision algorithms and intelligent image analysis to ensure high diagnostic accuracy.

Portable and Easy to Use

Compact and lightweight design, can be carried and used anywhere without the need for complex equipment.

Economical Solution

Low cost compared to traditional diagnostic solutions, making it an ideal option for resource-limited areas.

Multi-Language Support

Software interface that supports multiple languages to facilitate local and international use.
