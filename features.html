Features

Non-Invasive Diagnosis

Our system provides non-contact thermal imaging to support healthcare professionals in diagnosing vascular, muscular, and inflammatory conditions.

Embedded Technology

Fully embedded system design allows real-time thermal data acquisition, processing, and visualization using compact and portable devices.

High Precision

Advanced infrared sensors ensure accurate thermal measurements with fine resolution, enhancing clinical decision-making.

Medical Applications

Applicable to various medical fields including oncology, vascular disorders, rehabilitation, and early diagnosis of inflammatory diseases.
