What is ThermaViX?

ThermaViX is a smart, portable medical device based on infrared thermal imaging technology. It enables rapid, non-invasive, and non-contact detection of thermal anomalies in the human body. By integrating an advanced thermal camera with a software application powered by computer vision algorithms, the device aids in the early diagnosis of conditions such as inflammation, infections, or blood disorders even before traditional symptoms appear.

ThermaViX is compact, economical, and easy to use, making it an innovative and practical solution for various medical institutions, especially in resource-poor areas, where it saves valuable time, assists in immediate diagnosis, and improves health prevention.

What is Infrared Thermography?

Infrared thermography is a technique that uses sensitive cameras to measure the thermal radiation emitted from the body and convert it into a colored thermal image. This technique allows for the detection of subtle temperature differences on the skin surface, which may indicate the presence of inflammation or other health problems.

This technology was developed in the early 20th century and initially used for military and industrial purposes, then later entered the medical field and significantly evolved with the advent of digital cameras and smart processors.
