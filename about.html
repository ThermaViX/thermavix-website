About ThermaViX

Our Mission

ThermaViX aims to transform medical diagnostics through non-invasive infrared thermography technology. By utilizing advanced embedded systems and precise thermal sensors, we enable healthcare professionals to detect, monitor, and manage numerous health conditions with greater accuracy and patient comfort.

Technology

Our system combines high-resolution thermal imaging with intelligent data analysis to offer real-time insights into vascular diseases, tumors, inflammation, and metabolic disorders. The embedded design ensures portability, affordability, and ease of integration into clinical environments.
